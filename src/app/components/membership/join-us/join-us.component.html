With over 150 members and still counting, Nigeria LP Gas Association (NLPGA) is the umbrella body of all Stakeholders in the LP Gas sector in Nigeria.

It is a Public / Private initiative established in line with the directive of the Federal Government of Nigeria as advised by the World Bank in the year 2003.

The primary objective of the Association is to promote the use of LP Gas in Nigeria at affordable costs. It aims at protecting the interests of the LP Gas industry and the entire Nigerian socio-economic environment at large, through public policy advocacy, creation and facilitation of commercial and industrial opportunities, provision of business development services and observance of the highest standard of operational and business ethics.

It also represents the opinion of the stakeholders on all matters, which impact on the investment environment and the Nigerian socio-economy as a whole.

As part of our ongoing activities, NLPGA will continue to build partnerships with important entities such as the Federal and State Ministries in Nigeria, Government Agencies and International Bodies

Our staff are also active with networks of member company representatives around Nigeria

Our Annual NLPGA Conference is an avenue where our members, Industry leaders and other stakeholders in the local and international LPG sector meet, learn and make business.

Our wide range of services to Members also include annual global LP Gas Statistics, Nigeria LP Gas Statistics, Members-only reports, Newsletters and staff answers to specific questions on all matters related to the LP Gas business, from production to end-use.

Our Mission & Vision

NLPGA will actively champion the competitiveness and utilisation of Liquefied Petroleum Gas by promoting sound policy development, capacity development and best practices towards optimising the economics of the entire gas value chain, while emphasising environmental sustainability and safety.

NLPGA will be the most influential and independent non-profit organization, in effectively advancing the role of Nigerian Liquefied Petroleum Gas as the platform for championing the development of the gas industry in Nigeria.
